Who We Are

Building the Future
One Child at a Time

1-Litre Group of Schools is one of Nigeria's leading private educational institutions, combining globally recognised academic standards with deep-rooted African values. We believe every child carries infinite potential — our job is to unlock it.

Our campuses run from Crèche through Senior Secondary, offering a seamless, nurturing journey of growth, discovery, and achievement for every student.

🎯
Academic Excellence
Rigorous, enriching curriculum focused on critical thinking and real-world skills.
🤝
Character Formation
We build integrity, discipline, and empathy alongside academic achievement.
🔬
Modern Facilities
State-of-the-art STEM labs, libraries, sports courts, and digital classrooms.
🌍
Global Exposure
Exchange programmes, competitions, and collaborations beyond Nigeria's borders.
